## About the Collection

This is a collection of letters written by my maternal great grandparents, Margaret Skillbred and Selmer Halvorson, during the summer of 1945. At this time, they had met several times and began courting, choosing to build their trust in one another by maintaining their relationship over the "long" distance. Their letters are family treasures.
